ExploRatory Data Analytics (EDA) is a type of machine learning and data analysis technique that aims to extract actionable insights from large volumes of unstructured or raw data. By analyzing the relational, structural, and operational aspects of data, EDA provides valuable information for decision-making, business intelligence, and risk management purposes. The goal is to reveal hidden patterns, relationships, and trends in a dataset that can help organizations make informed decisions, identify opportunities, and optimize their operations.
