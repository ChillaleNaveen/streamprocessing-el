Change Management is a process of managing the impacts and outcomes of change in an organization or system, to ensure its success and minimize negative consequences. It encompasses a range of practices, techniques, and strategies designed to help organizations adapt and overcome challenges associated with changes, such as new products or services, organizational restructuring, mergers and acquisitions, and technology integration. The goal is to ensure that changes are implemented efficiently, effectively, and consistently across the organization, leading to long-term success and growth.
